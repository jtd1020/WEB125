Online Payments


Tyrrell County is pleased to offer our customers the convenience of online bill payment. We accept Visa, Mastercard, Discover, and American Express. Your account balance will be updated within moments to reflect your payment. You may choose to Go Paperless and receive ebill notifications and you can manage multiple accounts within your profile. Detailed payment and water usage history are also available. There is a convenience fee associated with paying your bill by credit/debit card of 3.5% per transaction. Please note that Tyrrell County Utilities does not receive the money for this convenience fee that is collected by the credit card processing company.
