Upcoming Events

At Topsail Beach, we want our visitors to enjoy their vacation. We offer a variety of events for folks from all walks of life. From thrilling surfing competitions to serene sunset yoga sessions, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Whether you're seeking family-friendly festivals, live music under the stars, or educational marine life tours, Topsail Beach promises unforgettable moments against the backdrop of stunning coastal beauty. Join us for a dynamic array of activities that celebrate the charm and diversity of beach life at Topsail.
